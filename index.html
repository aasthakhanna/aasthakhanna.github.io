FetchMe Android

FetchMe is an Android application that aims to connect dogs currently living in animal shelters with humans currently seeking out canine company. Shelters will upload profiles of each of their doggy residents onto FetchMe, filling out information such as the dog's name, a short personality description, and amount of time spent living at the shelter. Users can then browse through profiles put up by nearby shelters and swipe right on dogs they are interested in adopting, with the hopes that they use this information to contact target shelters and learn more about the adoption process. With widespread implementation and proper use, FetchMe will decrease the amount of homeless dogs across the nation and bring furry joy into the lives of thousands.
